Ichi's Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup diced vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or butter)
  • garlic to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil (or butter) in a large skillet, wok, or pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them lightly. Pour the eggs into the skillet and scramble them until almost fully cooked.
  3. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Add the garlic, vegetables and stir everything together.
  4. Pour the soy sauce over the rice mixture and stir well to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the chopped green onions.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious egg fried rice!

(Ichi's Note! Ichi hates washing dishes so this dish can all be made in one skillet, wok, or pot!)

Nya Nya Natto Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 pack natto (fermented soybeans)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the natto by stirring it well in its container to create a sticky texture. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chopped green onions and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Push the green onions to one side of the skillet and crack the eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir everything together.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, mixing well to evenly coat the rice.
  7. Add the prepared natto to the skillet and gently fold it into the rice mixture. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy your Nya Nya Natto Fried Rice!

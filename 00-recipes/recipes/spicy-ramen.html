Super Spicy Ramen

Ingredients:

  • Dry instant ramen noodles
  • 1 cup water or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of your choice of oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Brown sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water or chicken broth in a pot.
  2. Add the instant ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions (usually 3-4 minutes).
  3. In a separate pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the soy sauce, red chili flakes, and brown sugar to the pan. Stir well to combine.
  5. Once the noodles are cooked, drain any excess water and add the noodles to the pan with the sauce. Toss to coat the noodles evenly.
  6. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
  7. Enjoy your super spicy ramen!

(Ichi's note! Ichiban loves spicy foods! For those who don't, try making the dish without the chili flakes. Throwing in poached or soft boiled eggs are another great way to help flavor your ramen!

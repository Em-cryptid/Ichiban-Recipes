Purrfect Egg Drop Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil over medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with cold water until fully dissolved..
  3. Add the grated ginger, soy sauce, pepper, onion powder, and salt to the cornstarch mixture. Stir well to combine all the flavors.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until they are well mixed. Pour in a bit of water to help mix
  5. Add this mixture to the boiling broth, stirring continuously to avoid lumps
  6. Reduce the heat to low. Slowly pour the beaten eggs into the soup in a thin stream while stirring gently with a fork or chopsticks to create egg ribbons. The eggs will cook immediately
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your purrfect egg drop soup!

(Ichi's note! If your soup clouds you can add parmesan cheese on top of your serving and pretend it's Itallian Stracciatella! Don't ask how Ichi knows...)
